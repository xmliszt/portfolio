This is a place I have always wanted to put up in my personal website. I want it to be a free space where I can conduct all kinds of interesting experiments, being design and code related, for example, doing some cool CSS animations or pattern generations, or even some fun little JavaScript games. Or maybe a piece of video effects, or new ways of creating music? Or put up some interesting AI stuff. I want to make it a place where I can freely express my creativity and share it with the world. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do 😉

这是我一直想在我的个人网站上放置的地方。我希望它是一个自由的空间，我可以在这里进行各种有趣的实验，包括设计和代码相关的，例如做一些酷炫的CSS动画或图案生成，甚至一些有趣的小JavaScript游戏。或者一些视频特效，或者创作音乐的新方式？或者放一些有趣的AI相关的小实验。我希望它是一个我可以自由表达我的创造力并与世界分享的地方。希望你和我一样喜欢它😉
