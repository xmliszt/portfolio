**Joodle Pro Access Options & Pricing**:

- **Monthly Plan**: Billed monthly at the displayed price
- **Yearly Plan**: Billed annually at the displayed price
- **Lifetime**: One-time purchase that unlocks Joodle Pro forever

**Billing & Auto-Renewal**:

- Payment is charged to your Apple ID when you confirm the purchase
- **Auto-Renewal**: Monthly and yearly plans automatically renew at the same price unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the current period ends
- **Cancel Anytime**: You can cancel a monthly or yearly plan in Settings at any time to stop future charges
- **Free Access Period**: For first-time user, you will be automatically granted a 7-day Free Access period, in which you can experience all Pro features of Joodle for free!
