title: How do I access experimental features?
order: 0
---

**Experimental features are available exclusively to Joodle Pro subscribers.**

To access experimental features:

1. Make sure you have an active Joodle Pro subscription
2. Go to **Settings > Labs > Experimental Features**
3. Toggle on the experimental features you want to try

**Important Note**: Experimental features are fun little projects that we are testing. These features may affect performance or battery life on your device. Use them at your own discretion, and feel free to turn them off if you experience any issues.
