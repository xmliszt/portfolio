---
id: change-alarm-time
title: Can I change the time of an anniversary alarm?
order: 2
---

**To change when you will receive an anniversary alarm notification**:

1. Navigate to the day entry with the existing alarm.
2. Tap the **alarm icon** in the header on top left corner.
3. Adjust the time picker to your preferred time.
4. Tap **Update Alarm**.

The alarm will now notify you at the new time on that anniversary date.
