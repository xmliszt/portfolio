---
id: change-language
title: How do I change app language?
order: 5
---

**Joodle follows your device language by default:**

1. After updating, Joodle automatically uses your device's "Language & Region" setting.
2. If your device language is supported, the app switches automatically.

**To set language inside Joodle:**

1. Open Joodle and go to "Settings > General > Language".
2. Select one of the supported languages.

**To set language from iOS Settings:**

1. Open "Settings > Apps > Joodle > Language".
2. Choose your preferred language.

**Note:** If you do not see the new language immediately, fully close and reopen Joodle.

**Language priority:**

The in-app language setting takes priority over your device's system language. For example, if your device is set to English (`en`) but you choose Simplified Chinese (`zh-Hans`) inside Joodle, the app — including any Joodle widgets you have placed on your Home Screen — will display in Simplified Chinese.

To revert to following the system language, go to iOS **Settings > Apps > Joodle > Language** and select the language that has **Default**.
