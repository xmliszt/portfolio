Say hello to Joodle 2.3 — now with a wiggle!

**Wiggly Strokes**

Your doodles refuse to sit still. Flip on Wiggly Strokes and every line springs to life with a shaky, hand-drawn jitter that never stops squirming. Find it in **Settings > Labs > Experimental Features > Wiggly Strokes**.

**Share the Wiggle**

Two new video share styles let you show off your doodle mid-wiggle — Wiggly Minimal and Wiggly Excerpt. Export them straight from the share sheet.

**Improvements & Bugfixes**

- Polished the share carousel's progress indicator for video styles.
