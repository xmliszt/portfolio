**Say hello to Joodle 2.7 — paint your year in rainbow, now in Türkçe 🌈**

### Rainbow Theme

A new Rainbow theme paints each month's doodles in its own hue, so a full year reads as a gentle spectrum. Switching themes is now instant — no more waiting on a refresh. Rainbow is a Joodle Pro theme. Find it under Settings > Customization > Theme Color.

### Türkçe

Joodle now speaks **Turkish**. Switch anytime in Settings.

### Improvements & Bugfixes

- Added a new free theme color, Indigo.
- Optimized theme color change, now it is instant!
- Optimized the **Time Passing Backdrop** to save battery.
- Various optimizations and minor stability fixes.
