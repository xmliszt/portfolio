**Note Editor Gets a Full-Screen Upgrade**

Notes now open in a dedicated full-screen editor — no more cursor jumping, no more cramped inline text boxes. Just a clean space to write. And yes, you can comfortably write long diary now!

**New Features**

You can now select previous year of doodles to share.

**Improvements & Bugfixes**

- Fixed cursor jumping while typing in note editors
- Fixed split view snap positions and header layout
- Fixed inconsistent stroke width on share cards for weekly and monthly views
- Improved timeline scrubbing responsiveness
- Fixed placeholder icon incorrectly showing on entries without a doodle
