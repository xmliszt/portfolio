Say hello to Joodle 2.0 — our biggest update yet!

**Trace from a Reference**

Bring real life into your doodles. Capture anything with your camera — or import a photo from your library — then trace right on top of it on the canvas. You can also choose to save captured references to your photo album. A new interactive tutorial shows you how the first time.

**Quick Actions & Shortcuts**

Long-press the Joodle icon for the new "Today" quick action, and the Today shortcut now opens the canvas directly so you can start drawing instantly.

**Improvements & Bugfixes**

- Smoother canvas dismiss with a clear saving state
- Expanded and refined localization across the app
- Improved camera stability and capture reliability
- Fixed the reminder sheet animation
- Fixed inspiration prompt text wrapping
