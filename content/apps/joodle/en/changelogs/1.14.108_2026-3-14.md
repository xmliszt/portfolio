Joodle now supports multiple languages! Added **Simplified Chinese** and **Korean**. Will add more languages in the future!

**How to switch language?**

- After update, Joodle should automatically follow your device's "Language & Region" to set the language.
- If you still want to customize your language, you can do so via:
  - In app: Go to "Settings > General > Language" to choose a supported language.
  - In system app setting: Go to "Settings > Apps > Joodle > Language" to choose a preferred language.

**Improvements & Bugfix**

- Now you can backtrack on existing doodles.
- Also fixed the issue that non-dynamic island device unable to save doodle via dismissing the bottom sheet.
