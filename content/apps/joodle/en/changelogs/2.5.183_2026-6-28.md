### Zoom Your Camera Reference

Capturing a reference just got more precise. Pinch to zoom, or use the new vertical slider with its native-style ruler — from 0.5x ultra-wide (if your device supports it) all the way in to 10x. It even works with native Camera Control on supported iPhones. Set your dominant hand (during onboarding or in Settings > Interactions) and the slider moves to the edge that's the most comfortable for you to reach.

### A Living Time Passing Backdrop

Try the updated Time Passing Backdrop experimental feature — playful blobs that slosh with your device's tilt, draining gently as the day goes by. Turn it on in **Experimental Features**.

### 繁體中文

Joodle now speaks **Traditional Chinese**. Switch anytime in Settings.
