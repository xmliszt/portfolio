**Welcome to Joodle!** ✨

This is the very first release, and we’re excited to share the foundation of your visual daily journal.

**Core Features**

- Create a daily Joodle to capture moods, moments, or thoughts in seconds
- Draw freely with a simple, distraction-free canvas
- No art skills required — every doodle tells a story

**Visual Timeline**

- View your entire year at a glance with the grid
- Watch your days come together as a personal visual timeline
- Relive memories through tiny Joodles, one day at a time

**Anniversary Countdown**

- Add meaningful future dates
- Count down to moments that matter
- Optional reminders so you never miss an important day

**Widgets & System Integration**

- Add Joodle widgets to your **Home Screen**, **Lock Screen**, and **StandBy**
- Quick access via **Siri Shortcuts** and Spotlight Search

**iCloud Sync**

- Securely sync your Joodles across all your Apple devices
- Your memories stay private and always yours

**Personalization**

- Choose from vibrant color themes to make Joodle yours

**Sharing & Export**

- Export individual Joodles or your entire year

**Privacy First**

- No accounts required
- No tracking, no ads
- Your data stays on your devices, synced only via iCloud

Thank you for starting your journey with Joodle.  
Your year, in Joodles.
