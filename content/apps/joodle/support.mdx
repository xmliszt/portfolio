## Need Help?

We're here to help you get the most out of Joodle! Whether you have questions, feedback, or need assistance, there are several ways to get support.

## In-App Help

**Joodle has a built-in FAQ section that covers the most common questions.** You can access it directly from the app:

**Settings → Need Help? → Frequently Asked Questions**

The in-app FAQ covers topics including:

- **Subscription** - Details about Joodle Pro, billing, cancellation, and family sharing
- **iCloud Sync** - How to enable sync, troubleshooting sync issues, and data backup
- **Getting Started** - Creating Joodles, navigation, the scrubbing gesture, and editing entries
- **Reminders** - Setting up anniversary reminders, managing notifications, and troubleshooting
- **Widgets** - Adding widgets to your Home Screen and customization options
- **Sharing** - How to share beautiful Joodle cards and remove watermarks

## Quick Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Joodles not syncing?**

1. Ensure you have an active Joodle Pro subscription
2. Check that iCloud is enabled: Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Saved to iCloud → Joodle
3. Enable sync in the Joodle app: Settings → iCloud Sync
4. Restart the app after changing sync settings

**Not receiving reminder notifications?**

1. Go to iOS Settings → Notifications → Joodle
2. Enable "Allow Notifications"
3. Check that Focus Mode isn't blocking Joodle

**Widget not updating?**

1. Open the Joodle app (widgets refresh when the app is opened)
2. Wait a few minutes for iOS to update
3. Try removing and re-adding the widget

For more detailed help, check the in-app FAQ or reach out through any of the contact methods above.
